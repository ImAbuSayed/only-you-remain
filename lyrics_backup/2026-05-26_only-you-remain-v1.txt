[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The neon sign is buzzing, but the world outside is gone
It’s just the two of us here with the radio left on
I feel your midnight whisper pull me closer to the flame
When all the dust has settled, only you remain

[Verse 1]
We took the long way home down a dusty gravel road
With your head upon my shoulder and no place we had to go
The shadows in the dashboard light were dancing on your skin
I knew right in that moment I would let this feeling in

[Pre-Chorus]
There's a fire in the quiet, there's a secret in the dark
I can feel the heavy rhythm beating wildly in my heart
Every breath you take is drawing me across the final line
I just want to close the distance, I just want to make you mine

[Chorus]
Oh, the stars could fall from heaven and the rivers could run dry
But I’d still be right here getting lost inside your eyes
You’re the whiskey in my glass, you're the shelter in the rain
When the morning comes to find us, only you remain

[Post-Chorus]
Only you remain, running through my veins
(Only you) In the beautiful and crazy, only you remain

[Verse 2]
I pour another drink and watch you moving to the sound
Of a slow acoustic record gently spinning round and round
You cast a little smile my way, a promise in the room
And all the heavy burdens of the day are fading soon

[Bridge]
